Ging-earl Grey Brownies
===============

A cup of tea if tea was solid and made of chocolate.

Stuff:
-----
Wet ingredients:
1 c granulated sugar
1 c brown sugar 
1/2 c neutral oil
1/2 c melted butter 
2 tsp bergamot extract 
4 eggs

Dry ingredients:
1 c all purpose flour 
1/2 tsp kosher salt
2/3 c cocoa powder 
1 tsp baking powder 
1 tsp espresso powder 
1 tsp ground cardamom

Mix-ins:
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup chopped crystallized ginger 

Directions:
----------
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
Prepare a 9 by 13 metal pan; grease and line with parchment paper.
Get two bowls.
Mix the dry ingredients together in one bowl.
Mix the wet ingredients together in another bowl.
Mix the wet and dry ingredients together.
Fold in the mix-ins.
Pour contents into prepared pan.
Bake for 25 minutes.
Let cool fully before cutting.
Eat.
