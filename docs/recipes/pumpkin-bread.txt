Pumpkin Bread
===============

Autumnal sweet bread
A subtle spicy delight
Memory of spring

Stuff:
-----
Wet ingredients:
1 can pumpkin
3/4 c butter, softened 
1 tsp vanilla extract 
2 eggs

Dry ingredients:
2 c all purpose flour 
2 c sugar 
1.5 tsp baking powder 
1 tsp ginger 
1 tsp cinnamon 
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cloves

Directions:
----------
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit.
Mix the wet ingredients together, then add in spices and baking powder, then sugar, then flour. 
OPTIONAL: If Gregg is around, add one full bag of chocolate chips.
Grease a 9 by 13 metal pan. 
Put batter into the pan.
Bake for 40 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean when stabbed.
